# 5. Abstract Concepts / Reasoning (抽象概念與思維)

`Sources: Atlantis, SG1, book, random, unknown`

## comprehend

`理解;領會` (v.) `[book][random]`

> I struggled to comprehend the complex theory at first.

我起初很難理解這個複雜的理論。

## premise

`前提;假設` (n.) `[book]`

> The premise of the argument is everyone deserves equal rights.

這個論點的前提是每個人都應享有平等的權利。

## proposition

`提案;論點;命題` (n.) `[book]`

`利害關係;賭注` (n.) `[book][random]`

`憤世嫉俗` (n.) `[book]`

## skepticism

`懷疑態度` (n.) `[book]`

`不太可能的` (adj.) `[book]`

`傾向;癖好` (n.) `[book][random]`
