# 9. Idioms / Phrases (慣用語)

`Sources: Atlantis, SG1, movie, Revex, taiwan`

## coop up

`把...關起來` (phr.) `[Atlantis]`

> He was cooped up in a tiny room for days.

他被關在小房間裡好幾天。

## blind commitment

`盲目的承諾` (n.) `[Atlantis]`

> Signing without reading is a blind commitment.

未讀內容就簽字是盲目的承諾。

## thing go south

`事情變糟` (phr.) `[Atlantis]`

> When the mission went south, they had to retreat.

當任務出問題時,他們只好撤退。

## pipe dream

`白日夢;空想` (n.) `[Atlantis]`

> Traveling to the stars is no pipe dream.

到星星旅行並非空想。

## dog tag

`身份識別牌` (n.) `[Atlantis]`

> Soldiers wear dog tags around their necks.

士兵們脖子上掛著身份牌。

## snake oil

`萬靈油;騙人的東西` (n.) `[Atlantis]`

> Don't believe his promises; they're just snake oil.

別相信他的承諾,那只是騙人的玩意。

## out of the blue

`出乎意料地` (phr.) `[Atlantis]`

> The visitor arrived out of the blue.

訪客出乎意料地出現了。

## Let the cat out of the bag

`無意中洩露秘密` (phr.) `[Atlantis]`

> He let the cat out of the bag about the surprise party.

他無意中洩露了驚喜派對的秘密。

## a long shot

`孤注一擲;機會渺茫` (phr.) `[Atlantis]`

> Trying to fix the engine alone is a long shot.

獨自修理引擎是個渺茫的嘗試。

## next of kin

`最近的親屬` (n.) `[Atlantis]`

> The army notified his next of kin of his death.

軍方通知他的最近親屬關於他的死訊。

## shut-in

`閉關者;足不出戶的人` (n.) `[Atlantis]`

> He has become a shut-in since the incident.

自從那事件後他變得足不出戶。

## knock it off

`住手;停止` (phr.) `[movie]`

> Knock it off, guys — we need to focus on the mission.

住手,夥伴們——我們需要專注於任務上。

## this is not a drill

`這不是演習;這是真的` (phr.) `[movie]`

> Listen up — this is not a drill, we have a real emergency.

聽著——這不是演習,我們遇到真正的緊急狀況。

## learning the ropes

`學習新事物的竅門` (phr.) `[Revex]`

> She is still learning the ropes at her new job.

她仍在熟悉新工作的竅門。

## rabbit hole

`深入探究的迷宮` (phr.) `[Revex]`

> The investigation led us down a rabbit hole of unexpected details.

這項調查把我們帶進了一個出乎意料細節的迷宮。

## train has left the station

`木已成舟,錯過時機` (phr.) `[Revex]`

> The deadline is tomorrow, so the train has left the station.

截止日期就在明天,那個想法已經錯過時機了。

## back in the day

`從前;以前` (phr.) `[bodyhealth]`

> Back in the day, people used cold cream as a basic moisturiser.

從前,人們用冷霜作為基本的保濕品。

## come home in a box

`裝在棺材裡回家(陣亡)` (phr.) `[bodyhealth]`

> Soldiers fear coming home in a box from the war.

士兵害怕從戰場上裝進棺材回家。

## it's cool

`沒關係;可以的` (phr.) `[bodyhealth]`

> If you are too tired to wash your face tonight, it's cool, just splash some water.

如果你今晚太累不想洗臉,沒關係,潑點水就好。

## second thought

`重新考慮;猶豫` (phr.) `[bodyhealth]`

> I had a second thought about using that harsh scrub.

我對使用那款刺激的去角質霜起了猶豫。

## at hand

`即將來臨` (phr.) `[random]`

> The deadline is at hand.

截止日期即將來臨。

## in retrospect

`回顧起來` (phr.) `[unknown]`

> In retrospect, taking that job was the right decision.

回顧起來,接受那份工作是正確的決定。
