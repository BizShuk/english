# 13. Law / Politics (法律與政治)

`Sources: Atlantis, SG1, random`

## assign blame

`歸咎;指責` (phr.) `[SG1]`

> The committee refused to assign blame for the failed mission.

委員會拒絕為失敗的任務指責任何人。

## sanction

`制裁;批准` (n.) `[SG1]`

> The council voted to impose economic sanctions on the rogue nation.

議會投票決定對流氓國家實施經濟制裁。

## subpoenaed

`被傳喚;被傳訊` (v.) `[SG1]`

> He was subpoenaed to testify before the Senate committee.

他被傳喚到參議院委員會作證。

## abduct

`綁架;誘拐` (v.) `[SG1][random]`

> The aliens attempted to abduct the scientists for study.

外星人試圖綁架那些科學家以供研究。

## infiltrate

`滲透;潛入` (v.) `[SG1]`

> The spy was sent to infiltrate the enemy's command center.

那名間諜被派去滲透敵方指揮中心。

## preposterous

`荒謬的;荒誕的` (adj.) `[SG1][book]`

> His preposterous claims were dismissed by the review board.

他那荒謬的說法被審查委員會駁回。

## jeopardy

`危險;風險` (n.) `[SG1]`

> Putting the team in jeopardy was not part of the plan.

讓小隊陷入危險並不在計畫之中。

## comply

`服從;遵守` (v.) `[SG1]`

> All personnel must comply with the new security protocols.

所有人員都必須遵守新的安全規定。

## amnesty

`特赦;大赦` (n.) `[Atlantis]`

> The king granted amnesty to the political prisoners.

國王特赦了政治犯。

## custody

`監護;拘留` (n.) `[Atlantis]`

> The suspect is in police custody.

嫌疑人現由警方拘留。

## appellate

`上訴的;上訴法院的` (adj.) `[Atlantis]`

> The appellate court reversed the original decision.

上訴法院推翻了原判。

## probationary

`試用的;緩刑的` (adj.) `[Atlantis]`

> The new recruit is on a probationary period.

新兵正處於試用期。

## impudence

`無禮;厚顏無恥` (n.) `[SG1]`

> He had the impudence to question the general's orders in public.

他竟厚顏無恥地當眾質疑將軍的命令。
