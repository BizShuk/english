# 10. Food & Cooking (食物與烹飪)

`Sources: cuisin, taiwan, movie, Mac2019`

## mirepoix

`蔬菜基底(洋蔥、胡蘿蔔、芹菜丁)` (n.) `[cuisin]`

> A classic mirepoix forms the flavor base of many French soups.

經典的蔬菜基底是許多法式湯品風味的根基。

## julienne

`切絲(切成細條)` (n./v.) `[cuisin]`

> Cut the carrots into julienne strips for a delicate presentation.

把胡蘿蔔切成細絲,擺盤更精緻。

## brunoise

`切丁(極細小丁)` (n./v.) `[cuisin]`

> Dice the vegetables into a fine brunoise for the garnish.

將蔬菜切成極細小丁作裝飾用。

## chiffonade

`切絲(將葉菜捲起切細絲)` (n./v.) `[cuisin]`

> Roll the basil leaves tightly and slice them into a chiffonade.

把羅勒葉捲緊,再切成細絲。

## bouquet garni

`香料束` (n.) `[cuisin]`

> Remember to remove the bouquet garni before serving the stew.

上菜前別忘了把香料束撈出來。

## roux

`油麵糊` (n.) `[cuisin]`

> Whisk the roux slowly until it turns a pale golden color.

慢慢攪拌油麵糊,直到呈現淡金黃色。

## soffritto

`義式蔬菜基底` (n.) `[cuisin]`

> Soffritto is the Italian cousin of the French mirepoix.

義式蔬菜基底是法式蔬菜基底的義大利兄弟版本。

## blanch

`汆燙;川燙` (v.) `[cuisin]`

> Blanch the green beans for two minutes, then transfer to ice water.

把四季豆汆燙兩分鐘,再放入冰水中。

## marinate

`醃漬;浸泡` (v.) `[cuisin]`

> Let the chicken marinate in lemon juice for at least an hour.

讓雞肉在檸檬汁中至少醃漬一小時。

## thyme

`百里香` (n.) `[cuisin]`

> Sprinkle fresh thyme over the roasted chicken for extra aroma.

在烤雞上撒點新鮮百里香增添香氣。

## rosemary

`迷迭香` (n.) `[cuisin]`

> Tuck a sprig of rosemary under the skin of the lamb.

把一小枝迷迭香塞進羊羔肉皮下。

## cumin

`小茴香;孜然` (n.) `[cuisin]`

> Toasted cumin seeds add a warm, earthy flavor to the stew.

烘烤過的小茴香籽能為燉菜增添溫暖的泥土香。

## fennel seed

`茴香籽` (n.) `[cuisin]`

> Fennel seeds are often used in Italian sausage recipes.

茴香籽常用於義式香腸的食譜中。

## turnip

`蕪菁` (n.) `[cuisin]`

> Roast the turnip with olive oil until it turns golden brown.

把蕪菁用橄欖油烤至金黃色。

## leek

`青蔥(西洋蔥)` (n.) `[cuisin]`

> Rinse the leek thoroughly to remove any grit between the layers.

徹底清洗青蔥,去除葉層間的泥沙。

## peppercorn

`胡椒粒` (n.) `[cuisin]`

> Crack the peppercorns just before seasoning the steak.

在調味牛排前現磨胡椒粒。

## buckwheat

`蕎麥` (n.) `[cuisin]`

> Buckwheat noodles are a popular choice in Japanese cuisine.

蕎麥麵是日本料理中很受歡迎的選擇。

## donning / doffing

`穿上/脫下(廚師服裝)` (v.) `[cuisin]`

> The chef is donning a fresh apron before service.

主廚在營業前換上一條乾淨的圍裙。

## turnip cake

`蘿蔔糕` (n.) `[taiwan]`

> I had pan-fried turnip cake with soy sauce for breakfast at the night market.

我早餐在夜市吃了淋上醬油的香煎蘿蔔糕。

## meat floss

`肉鬆` (n.) `[taiwan]`

> She spread meat floss on warm toast for a quick breakfast.

她在溫熱的吐司上抹了肉鬆當作快速的早餐。

## mullet roe

`烏魚子` (n.) `[taiwan]`

> Mullet roe is often served sliced with garlic and white wine.

烏魚子常切片搭配大蒜和少許白酒一起享用。

## milkfish

`虱目魚` (n.) `[taiwan]`

> Milkfish is commonly used in Taiwanese congee and soups.

虱目魚常用於台灣的粥品和湯品中。

## sugar apple

`釋迦` (n.) `[taiwan]`

> Sugar apple has sweet, creamy flesh that tastes tropical.

釋迦有著甜美綿密、帶有熱帶風味的果肉。

## wax apple

`蓮霧` (n.) `[taiwan]`

> A crisp wax apple is perfect on a hot summer afternoon.

清脆的蓮霧在炎熱的夏日午後最對味。

## candied fruit

`蜜餞` (n.) `[taiwan]`

> These candied fruits are a traditional snack in Taiwan.

這些蜜餞是台灣的傳統零食。

## sorghum

`高粱` (n.) `[taiwan]`

> Sorghum is used to make a strong traditional liquor in Kinmen.

高粱用於釀造金門的傳統烈酒。

## pomegranate

`石榴` (n.) `[taiwan]`

> Pomegranate seeds add a juicy burst to any salad.

石榴籽能為沙拉增添多汁的口感。

## cricket

`蟋蟀;食用昆蟲` (n.) `[taiwan]`

> Fried crickets are a crunchy, protein-rich snack in some Asian markets.

炸蟋蟀在亞洲一些市場是酥脆且富含蛋白質的點心。

## nougat

`牛軋糖` (n.) `[taiwan]`

> Taiwanese nougat is famous for its soft, chewy texture.

台灣牛軋糖以柔軟Q彈的口感聞名。

## kosher

`符合猶太戒律的` (adj.) `[taiwan]`

> This restaurant serves kosher food prepared according to Jewish law.

這家餐廳供應符合猶太教規的潔食料理。

## patty

`肉餅;漢堡排` (n.) `[movie][bodyhealth]`

> She made a vegetable patty for lunch today.

她今天做了一個蔬菜漢堡排當午餐。
