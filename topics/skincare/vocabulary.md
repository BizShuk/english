# 18. Skincare (皮膚保養)

`Source: bodyhealth`

## exfoliate

`去角質` (v.) `[bodyhealth]`

> You should exfoliate your face twice a week to remove dead skin cells.

你應該每週去兩次角質,去除老廢角質。

## hydrating

`保濕的` (adj.) `[bodyhealth]`

> This hydrating cream keeps my skin soft all day long.

這款保濕霜讓我的皮膚整天保持柔軟。

## sebaceous filament

`皮脂管絲` (n.) `[bodyhealth]`

> A sebaceous filament is a tiny collection of oil in a pore.

皮脂管絲是毛孔中一小團皮脂的堆積。

## congest

`堵塞;阻塞` (v.) `[bodyhealth]`

> Heavy creams can congest your pores if you do not cleanse properly.

如果清潔不當,厚重的乳霜可能會堵塞你的毛孔。

## crusty

`結痂的;硬皮的` (adj.) `[bodyhealth]`

> The scab became crusty after it dried in the sun.

結痂處在陽光下曬乾後變得硬硬的。

## gyps (gypsum)

`石膏` (n.) `[bodyhealth]`

> Gypsum is often used in face masks to firm the skin.

石膏常用於面膜中以緊緻肌膚。

## vulcanized

`經硫化處理的` (adj.) `[bodyhealth]`

> Vulcanized rubber is used to make durable spa equipment.

硫化橡膠用於製造耐用的 Spa 設備。

## kryptonite

`弱點;剋星` (n.) `[bodyhealth]`

> Sugar is my kryptonite when it comes to clear skin.

談到淨透肌膚,糖就是我的剋星。
