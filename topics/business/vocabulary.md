# 16. Business / Management (商業與管理)

`Sources: manager, random, Revex, movie`

## echelon

`階層` (n.) `[manager]`

> Senior managers at the highest echelon set the company's long-term strategy.

最高階層的高階經理制定了公司的長期策略。

## rapport

`和諧關係;融洽` (n.) `[manager]`

> Building rapport with new colleagues helps you work more effectively.

與新同事建立融洽關係有助於更有效率地合作。

## fallback plan

`備用方案` (n.) `[manager]`

> We should prepare a fallback plan in case the main launch is delayed.

我們應該準備備用方案以防主要推出計畫被延遲。

## inflection point

`轉折點` (n.) `[manager]`

> Launching the new product was an inflection point for the company's growth.

推出新產品是公司成長的轉折點。

## ramification

`後果;影響` (n.) `[manager]`

> You need to consider the ramifications of changing the team structure.

你需要考慮調整組織結構的後果。

## stifle

`扼殺;抑制` (v.) `[manager]`

> Too many approval layers stifle innovation within the organization.

太多的審核層級會扼殺組織內的創新。

## preconception

`先入為主的觀念` (n.) `[manager]`

> Try to set aside preconceptions when you evaluate a new idea.

評估新想法時,試著放下先入為主的觀念。

## clutch

`關鍵時刻的` (adj.) `[manager]`

> She made a clutch decision that saved the project from failing.

她在緊要關頭做出了一個關鍵決定。

## network of trust

`信任網絡` (n.) `[Revex]`

> A strong network of trust is essential for long-term partnerships.

穩固的信任網絡對長期合作關係至關重要。

## daisy-chain

`菊輪式串接` (v.) `[Revex]`

> We daisy-chain the sensors so each one passes data to the next.

我們將感測器菊輪式串接,讓每個裝置將資料傳給下一個。

## acquihire

`人才收購` (n.) `[random]`

> The startup was acquired as an acquihire.

這家新創公司是作為人才收購而被併購。

## turnaround time

`週轉時間` (n.) `[random]`

> Our turnaround time for orders is 24 hours.

我們的訂單週轉時間為 24 小時。

## rightsize

`精簡` (v.) `[random]`

> The company had to rightsize its workforce.

公司必須精簡員工。

## in-house

`內部的` (adj.) `[random]`

> We developed the software using an in-house team.

我們使用內部團隊開發了該軟體。
