# 7. Character / Personality (性格與人格)

`Sources: Atlantis, SG1, manager, movie`

## prudent

`謹慎的;明智的` (adj.) `[Atlantis][SG1][manager]`

> A prudent leader plans for every outcome.

謹慎的領導人會為每種結果做好準備。

## callous

`冷酷的;無情的` (adj.) `[Atlantis]`

> His callous remarks hurt everyone in the room.

他冷酷的言論傷害了在場的每個人。

## cantankerous

`脾氣壞的` (adj.) `[Atlantis]`

> The cantankerous old sailor argued with everyone.

脾氣暴躁的老水手跟每個人吵架。

## brusque

`唐突的;粗暴的` (adj.) `[Atlantis]`

> His brusque manner offended the visitors.

他唐突的態度冒犯了訪客。

## recluse

`隱士;隱居者` (n.) `[Atlantis]`

> The hermit lives as a recluse in the mountains.

那位隱士在山中過著隱居生活。

## dupe

`受騙者;傻瓜` (n.) `[Atlantis]`

> He was the dupe of a clever swindler.

他成了狡猾騙子的受害者。

## rogue

`流氓;無賴` (n./adj.) `[Atlantis]`

> The rogue captain answered to no one.

那個無賴艦長不聽任何人命令。

## renegade

`叛徒;變節者` (n.) `[Atlantis]`

> The renegade betrayed his own people.

叛徒背叛了自己人。

## rascal

`搗蛋鬼` (n.) `[Mac2019]`

> That little rascal stole cookies from the jar again.

那個小搗蛋鬼又從罐子裡偷了餅乾。

## imbecile

`愚人` (n.) `[Mac2019]`

> He felt like an imbecile for forgetting her birthday.

他因為忘了她的生日而感到自己像個傻瓜。

## despicable

`卑鄙的;可憎的` (adj.) `[SG1]`

> His despicable actions earned him the hatred of his crew.

他那卑鄙的行為招來了隊友的憎恨。

## insubordinate

`不服從的` (adj.) `[SG1]`

> The insubordinate officer was court-martialed for his actions.

那名不服從的軍官因他的行為而受到軍法審判。

## formidable

`可怕的;難以對付的` (adj.) `[SG1]`

> Apophis is a formidable enemy who commands a vast fleet.

阿波菲斯是個率領龐大艦隊的可怕敵手。

## cocky

`自大的` (adj.) `[Mac2019]`

> The young player was too cocky after his first win.

這名年輕球員在首勝後變得過於自大。

## snarky

`尖酸刻薄的` (adj.) `[movie]`

> She made a snarky comment about the film.

她對電影發表了一段尖酸刻薄的評論。

## machismo

`男子氣概;大男人主義` (n.) `[manager]`

> His machismo made it hard for him to admit mistakes in front of the team.

他的大男人主義讓他難以在團隊面前承認錯誤。
