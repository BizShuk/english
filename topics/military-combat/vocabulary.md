# 2. Military / Combat / Strategy (軍事與戰鬥)

`Sources: Atlantis, SG1, Mac2019, random`

## stealth mode

`隱形模式` (n.) `[Atlantis]`

> The submarine switched to stealth mode to avoid detection.

潛水艇切換到隱形模式以避免被偵測。

## contingent

`後備部隊;視情況而定` (n.) `[Atlantis]`

> A contingent of soldiers was sent to reinforce the outpost.

一批後備部隊被派去增援前哨基地。

## sitting duck

`易受攻擊的目標` (n.) `[Atlantis]`

> Without shields, the vessel was a sitting duck.

沒有防護罩,這艘船就成了容易攻擊的目標。

## ramming

`衝撞` (n.) `[Atlantis]`

> The pilot tried ramming the enemy fighter to stop it.

飛行員試圖衝撞敵機來阻止它。

## distress call

`求救訊號` (n.) `[Atlantis]`

> The captain sent out a distress call after the ship was hit.

船長在船被擊中後發出了求救訊息。

## insurgency

`叛亂;暴動` (n.) `[SG1]`

> The team studied insurgency techniques used by rebel forces.

小隊研究了叛軍所使用的叛亂戰術。

## detainee

`被拘留者` (n.) `[SG1]`

> The detainee refused to give any information to the interrogators.

被拘留者拒絕向審問者透露任何資訊。

## rendition

`囚犯移送;引渡` (n.) `[SG1]`

> The government approved the rendition of the terrorist to face trial.

政府批准將那名恐怖分子移送受審。

## arsenal

`武器庫;兵工廠` (n.) `[SG1]`

> The enemy hid a massive arsenal in the mountain caves.

敵人把大量武器藏在山洞裡。

## stronghold

`堡壘;據點` (n.) `[SG1]`

> The rebels made the canyon their stronghold.

叛軍把這座峽谷當作他們的據點。

## incursion

`入侵;侵襲` (n.) `[SG1]`

> The incursion into enemy territory happened under cover of night.

在夜色的掩護下,他們入侵了敵方領土。

## ballistic

`彈道的;暴怒的` (adj.) `[SG1]`

> The missile went ballistic and arced high into the sky.

飛彈沿彈道路徑高高射入空中。

## firearm

`槍械;火器` (n.) `[SG1]`

> The soldier cleaned his firearm before going on patrol.

士兵在巡邏前清潔了他的槍械。

## smuggle

`走私;偷運` (v.) `[SG1]`

> They tried to smuggle weapons across the border.

他們試圖將武器走私過境。

## brawl

`鬥毆;喧鬧的打架` (n.) `[SG1][Mac2019]`

> A brawl broke out in the bar late at night.

深夜酒吧裡爆發了一場鬥毆。

## jurisdiction

`管轄權;司法權` (n.) `[Atlantis][SG1]`

> This matter falls outside our military jurisdiction.

這件事不在我們軍方的管轄範圍內。

## cull

`挑選;剔除` (v.) `[Atlantis]`

> They had to cull the weak animals from the herd.

他們必須從獸群中剔除瘦弱的動物。

## treat / treaty

`條約` (n.) `[Atlantis]`

> The two nations signed a peace treaty.

兩國簽署了和平條約。

## perimeter

`周邊;周界` (n.) `[Atlantis][Mac2019]`

> Soldiers guarded the perimeter of the base.

士兵守衛著基地的周邊。

## confiscate

`沒收;充公` (v.) `[Atlantis]`

> The authorities confiscated the smuggled goods.

當局沒收了走私貨物。

## rout

`擊潰` (v.) `[random]`

> The army managed to rout the enemy completely.

軍隊成功徹底擊潰了敵人。

## ceasefire

`停火` (n.) `[SG1]`

> The two sides agreed to a ceasefire after weeks of fighting.

雙方在數週交戰後同意停火。
